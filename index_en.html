Privacy Policy

Yojummal (the “App”) values your personal information and is committed to protecting it. The App complies with applicable laws and regulations related to personal data protection.

1. Personal Information We Collect

  • Account Management: Email address (for login and user verification)
  • Service Usage & Analytics: Device identifiers (ADID/IDFA), device model, operating system version
  • Payment Management: In-app purchase receipt data (actual payment method details are managed by the app stores)

2. Purpose of Use

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • User authentication and service provision (via Supabase)
  • In-app purchases and subscription status management (via RevenueCat)
  • Advertising delivery and prevention of fraudulent activity (via Google AdMob)
  • Service improvement and statistical analysis

3. Third-Party Services and Data Processing

To provide smooth and reliable services, the App uses third-party services. Personal data may be processed by the following service providers:

  • Supabase: Data storage and user authentication
  • RevenueCat: In-app subscriptions and payment status management
  • Google AdMob: Advertising services
  • Mapbox: Map and location-based services
  • Apple / Google: In-app payment processing

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected. Once the purpose has been achieved, the data is promptly deleted, unless retention is required by applicable laws.

5. User Rights

Users may view or update their personal information at any time and may request account deletion to exercise their rights as a data subject.
